  • Did you know?..


    • When you update your game your data does not get automatically transfered, yet (Though this may change in the new update V3.8.0)
    But in the meantime here is how to do this!
  • Firstly, go into the game's folder and locate show hidden files at the top of file explorer.
  • Then once done, you should see 3 new folders, locate " saves " (Open it).
  • Then locate " player_save " and copy it. NOTE: V2.7.0 or lower will not work!
  • After copying " player save " go back to the latest version and past it in the saves folder.
  • When a window comes up (if it dose) press " Replace file in destination "
  • Then just try and load the game up again and all of your data should be good to go!
